National Park Analysis

Summary

An analysis was done on whether the number of visitors to national parks depends on any of the following factors: the population the state is in, the temperature, or the price of gas.

Questions

  • 1. Does the number of visitors to a park depend on the population of the state the park is in?
  • 2. Does the number of visitors to a park depend on the temperature?
  • 3. Does the number of visitors to a park depend on the price of gas?
