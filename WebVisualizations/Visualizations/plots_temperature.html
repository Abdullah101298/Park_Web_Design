Temperature

Visualization #1

Summary

This plot shows a terrain map of the central and western parts of the US highlighting with a bubble for the location of each national park. The size of the bubble depends on the visitor count to the park. The color of the bubble depends on the average temperature in the park.

Visualization #2

Summary

These plots attempt to compare the temperature and the visitor count based on the different months of 2019. A correlation can be seen for an increase in visitor count in warmer weather excluding summer months where more people are on vacation.
